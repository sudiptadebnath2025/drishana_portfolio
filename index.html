About Me

On the journey of my space career despite all ups and downs and never turned back. Holding AIR 150 in GATE EE, pursuing masters at IISc Bangalore, and being a former intern at IIST Trivandrum, my interest lies in Power Electronics and Electric Drives.

As member of UN Space Generation Advisory Council's Project Member (Electric Power Subsystem) and delegate, I possess strong communication and leadership skills. Along with being a Maxar Awardee, I'm involved in citizen science activities with organizations like NASA.

Electrical Engineering Competitive Exams are my passion. I help students with their doubts through tutoring platforms and provide mentorship to students and young professionals for their careers.
